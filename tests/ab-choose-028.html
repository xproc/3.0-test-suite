p:choose-028 (AB)

Tests p:choose with multiple output ports where some are declared as producing sequences and some not. (ported from choose #007 of 1.0 test-suite)

Test ab-choose-028.xml is expected to pass.

The pipeline


        <p:declare-step xmlns:p="http://www.w3.org/ns/xproc" name="pipeline" version="3.0">
            <p:output port="result"/>
            <p:input port="source">
                <doc xmlns=""/>
            </p:input>
            <p:choose>
                <p:when test="1 = 1">
                    <p:output port="result"/>
                    <p:identity/>
                </p:when>
                <p:otherwise>
                    <p:output port="result" sequence="true"/>
                    <p:identity/>
                </p:otherwise>
            </p:choose>    
        </p:declare-step>
