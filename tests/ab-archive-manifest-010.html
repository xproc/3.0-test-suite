p:archive-manifest 010 (AB)

Tests p:archive-manifest: Relative-to creates absolute uris for href. Its resolve to base-uri of element.

Test ab-archive-manifest-010.xml is expected to pass.

The pipeline


      <p:declare-step xmlns:p="http://www.w3.org/ns/xproc" name="pipeline" version="3.0">
         <p:output port="result"/>
         <p:archive-manifest>
            <p:with-option name="relative-to" select="xs:anyURI('test/')" xml:base="http://xproc.org/ns/testsuite/3.0/"/>
            <p:with-input href="../documents/archive.zip"/>
         </p:archive-manifest>
      </p:declare-step>
